About Me

I’m a data scientist and machine learning engineer with a B.S. in Computer Science (Big Data concentration) and a minor in Statistics from the University of Wyoming. Currently, I’m pursuing a Master’s in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, expected March 2026. My academic background and professional work intersect across data engineering, AI systems development, and full-cycle ML pipelines.

I’ve worked for Sogeti (a Capgemini company) supporting enterprise-level Identity and Access Management, data integration projects, and QA automation liaison roles for Humana. I was honored to represent Sogeti at a Google GenAI Hackathon. I bring 6+ years of technical support and systems admin experience with a passion for troubleshooting, team collaboration, and customer success.

When I’m not solving technical problems, I’m out hiking, mountaineering, or snowboarding—or home writing music for guitar. I’m a lifelong learner and curious researcher with a love of statistics, generative models, and the occasional horror film binge. My approach is grounded, analytical, and creative—with a mindset of "build, test, refine."
