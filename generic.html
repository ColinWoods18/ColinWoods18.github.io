About Me

I also had a role serving as a Liaison between the Business Ruless, Manual Testing, and Automation teams at Humana Health Insurance. I am proficient with administrating all Operating Systems, Computer Networking, Audio/Visual support, imaging, and more!

In my free time I enjoy hiking, camping, backpacking and mountaineering, as well as snowboarding during the winter. I love playing the acoustic and electric guitar, and writing music. I am constantly trying to immerse myself in new subjects and absorb as much knowledge as I can. You can definitely catch me googling peer-reviewed research studies on my day off. I am a deep philosopher and one of the philosophies I subscribe to is, "Work hard. Play hard." I'm also a big horror film buff.
